💡 Study Tips for Success

📅

Plan Your Schedule

Allocate ~2 hours daily. At this pace, you can complete the entire roadmap in approximately 8–10 weeks.

🔁

Spaced Repetition

Review completed phases every 2 weeks. Use the formula sheet and revision checklist in Chapter 16 for quick recall.

✍️

Practice Problems

After each chapter, solve the quiz questions. For high-priority topics, solve GATE previous year questions immediately.

📝

Take Notes

Use the bookmark feature to save important pages. Write concise summaries in your own words for each chapter.

⏰

Time Your Practice

GATE gives ~2.7 minutes per question. Practice solving problems within this time limit, especially for search and logic.

🎯

Focus on High Priority

Phases 2 and 3 (Search + Logic/Reasoning) carry the most marks. Ensure you're rock-solid on these before moving to applications.
