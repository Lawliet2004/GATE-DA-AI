🎯 GATE DA Quick Info

📝 Exam Pattern

65 questions, 100 marks, 3 hours. Mix of MCQ and NAT (Numerical Answer Type).

📊 AI Weightage

Artificial Intelligence carries approximately 15–20 marks in the GATE DA paper.

🔥 High-Priority Topics

  • Search Algorithms (BFS, DFS, A*)
  • Logic & Inference
  • Bayesian Networks
  • ML Fundamentals
  • Neural Networks

💡 Preparation Tips

  • Master fundamentals before advanced topics
  • Practice previous year GATE questions
  • Focus on numerical problem solving
  • Use the formula sheet for quick revision
